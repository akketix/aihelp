# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-22_

The **AI Help Wiki** ("this wiki", "we") is a read-mostly reference site hosted on DigitalOcean App Platform and deployed from the public [akketix/aihelp](https://github.com/akketix/aihelp) repository. We aim to collect as little as possible. This policy explains the third parties we use and your choices.

## Advertising and cookies

To support hosting, this wiki displays ads served by **Google AdSense**. AdSense uses cookies and, in some regions, device identifiers and information about your activity on this and other sites, to serve and measure ads.

- **Google's cookies.** When ads load, Google may set cookies (e.g. `__gads`, the DoubleClick cookie `IDE` in some regions) to measure and personalise ad delivery. See [Google's advertising cookies page](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).
- **Consent (EEA & UK).** We use **Google's Consent Management Platform (CMP)** to show a consent message to visitors in the European Economic Area and the United Kingdom. For those visitors, advertising cookies and personalised ads are only set after consent via that message. You can change your choice at any time through the consent message or your browser settings. We do not run a custom consent banner.
- **Third-party vendors.** Google and its partners may use information collected through ads on this site to serve ads based on your visits here and elsewhere.

## How to opt out

- [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) — opt out of personalised advertising.
- [Network Advertising Alliance opt-out](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/).
- Your browser settings can block or delete cookies, or send a "Do Not Track" signal. Blocking ad cookies doesn't remove the wiki's content.

## Editing and user-submitted content

This wiki is read-mostly. To propose a change, use the **"Edit this page on GitHub"** link at the bottom of any page — it opens GitHub's editor and starts a **pull request** under your own GitHub account.

- **Submissions are public.** Because the repository is public, any edit you propose becomes a **public pull request and commit on GitHub**, visible to everyone and retained in the repository's history — even if the change is later closed or reverted. Please don't include personal information in edits.
- **Editing happens on GitHub.** The wiki itself has no custom submission form or backend that stores your personal data. Editing is governed by [GitHub's Privacy Policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) and occurs under your GitHub identity.

## What we don't do

We do not sell your information. Beyond what Google AdSense requires to serve ads (after consent where applicable) and what GitHub collects when you choose to edit, this wiki does not run a server-side analytics or account system that stores your personal data.

## Children's privacy

This wiki is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. AdSense is not configured for child-directed content.

## Changes to this policy

If we add or change the services we use, we will update this page with a new "Last updated" date. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

## Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue on [the GitHub repo](https://github.com/akketix/aihelp). For questions about Google's ad practices, contact Google directly.

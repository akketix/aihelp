# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 2026-07-21_

The **AI Help Wiki** ("this wiki", "we") is a read-mostly reference site hosted on DigitalOcean App Platform. We aim to collect as little as possible. This policy explains the one third-party service we use — Google AdSense — and the choices you have about it.

## Advertising and cookies

To support hosting, this wiki displays ads served by **Google AdSense**, a third-party ad vendor and a subsidiary of Google. AdSense uses cookies (small text files stored on your device) and, in some regions, device identifiers and information about your activity on this and other sites, to serve ads based on your interests.

- **Google's cookies.** When ads load, Google may set cookies such as `__gads` and the DoubleClick cookie (`IDE` in some regions) to measure and personalise ad delivery. See [Google's advertising cookies page](https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads).
- **Your consent.** We do not load the AdSense script until you accept cookies in the banner shown on your first visit. If you reject, the wiki stays ad-free and Google's ad cookies are not set. Your choice is stored locally in your browser under the key `mneurix-consent`.
- **Third-party vendors.** Google and its partners may use information they collect through ads on this site. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this site and other sites.

## How to opt out

- [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads) — opt out of personalised advertising.
- [Network Advertising Alliance opt-out](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/)
- Your browser's settings let you block or delete cookies, or send a "Do Not Track" signal. Blocking ad cookies does not remove the wiki's content.
- To withdraw consent later, clear the `mneurix-consent` entry from your browser's local storage for this domain and reload.

## What we don't do

This wiki is read-mostly. It has no server-side analytics, no account system, and no backend that stores your personal data. We do not sell or share your information, beyond what Google AdSense requires to serve ads after you have consented. The in-page edit submission form, when used, sends content you author to the repository's API for review — it does not track you or set ad cookies.

## Children's privacy

This wiki does not target children under 13, and AdSense is configured to serve non-personalised ads where required. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to this policy

If we add or change the services we use, we will update this page with a new "Last updated" date. Material changes will be reflected here before they take effect.

## Contact

For privacy questions about this wiki, open an issue on [the GitHub repo](https://github.com/akketix/aihelp). For questions about Google's ad practices, contact Google directly.
